Konect Agency provides a central hub where minority students pursuing an education in Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics can access support in a variety of areas geared toward the successful completion of College. Students can submit their information so companies can review in order to determine potential Scholarship awardees, interns or possibly employees. Students are able to get information on scholarships, workshops and more.

Mission Statment

The mission of Konect Agency is to improve significantly the psychological, career, financial and legal well being of minority students in the five boroughs of New York.
